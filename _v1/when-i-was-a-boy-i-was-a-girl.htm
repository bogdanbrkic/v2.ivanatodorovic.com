WHEN I WAS A BOY I WAS A GIRL

"EVERYONE DESERVES HAPPINESS IN LIFE ...HAPPINESSS"

￼￼

Sinopsis

“Ja kada sam bila klinac, bila sam klinka (When I Was a Boy, I Was a Girl) is an impressive documentary from Serbia. Finally, no clichés about sex workers! Ivana Todorovic portrays her transsexual friend who gives her very special, unpretentious access. A very intimate view, that gets very close. These are the films that we need in order to get closer to people who are so far from us.”

  • Maike Mia Hohne , program curator of Berlinale Shorts
  • 2013 premier at Berlin International Film Festival, Berlinale Shorts
  • 2013 Cinematic Achievement Award, Thess International Shorts Film Festival, Solun
  • 2013 Best Balkan Documentary Film, Dokufest, Prizren
  • 2013 Best Short Film, South Eastern European Film Festival, LA
  • 2013 Best National Documentary Film, Belgrade Shorts and Documentary Film Festival

+25, Selected festivals:

Sarajevo Film Festival, Santo Domingo Outfest, This Human World Film Festival, Cineeast, Festival du Film d’Europe Centrale , supported by Serbian Film Center, Fund Zenska Rekonstrukcija, Heartefact Fund

Film screened at various LGBT events.

Featuring Gordana Mitrovic; directed&shoot by Ivana Todorovic, edited by Jelena Maksimovic. music by Drasko Adzic,text written by Olga Dimitrijevic and Goca, sound design: Danijel Milosevic, poster design Nebojsa Petrovic, produced by Academic Film Center, Dom Kulture Studentski Grad and Ivana Todorovic supported by Serbian Film Center, Fund Zenska Rekonstrukcija, Heartefact Fund
