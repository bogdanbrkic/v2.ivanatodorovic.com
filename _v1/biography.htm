Ivana Todorovic was born in Belgrade, Yugoslavia. She is a director, shooter and co-producer of four short documentary films.  All of the films are geared toward promoting and producing social change. Her documentaries tackle such issues as gender and sexuality, homelessness, the education of Roma children living in squatter settlements in Belgrade and youth gun violence in New York City.

Films are used in educational settings, media campaigns and by various NGOs.

Ivana received her education at the Ethnology and Anthropology department at University of Belgrade, Ateliers Varan documentary film workshop founded by Jean Rouch, Peace Studies and The New Politics School, and also studied Documentary Media studies at The New School in NY. She was a selected participant at the 2010 New York Foundation for the Arts Immigrant Artist Project.

She is an experienced lecturer, was a speaker at TEDxAUK.
