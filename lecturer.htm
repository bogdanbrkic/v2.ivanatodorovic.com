LECTURER

ARTIST TALKS / MASTER CLASSES /
PRACTICAL HANDS-ON WORKSHOPS

I believe we can all contribute to a better society through communicating what is in our hearts, one way to do this is through documentary film. I give artist talks, master classes and practical hands-on workshops at universities, film festivals and community centers. My presentations involve screening examples of films I’ve made and applying practical analysis of what it means to be working as shooter and director. The students are familiarized with the history of documentary film, they learn about locating and dealing with themes and protagonists, as well as interview and shooting techniques. The presentations always engage and include student’s participation.

Previous talks, workshops and master classes:

  • TEDxAUK
  • Europe at Heart, Heinrich-Boll Stiftung European Union, Brussels
  • Documentary Club, American University, Blagoevgrad, Bulgaria
  • School for Visual Anthropology, NGO KSEA, Belgrade, Serbia
  • Kino Klub Split, Split, Croatia
  • Supetar Super Film Festival, Brac, Croatia
  • Migration Citizenship Education, Berlin, Germany
  • Gypsy/Roma Human Rights Film Festival, New York
  • International Institute of Social History, Graz, Austria
